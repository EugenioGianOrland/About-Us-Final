About Us

Our website is a Suicide prevention website that helps people that is suffering from depression. And we are the leader in suicide prevention and mental health crisis care. Since its inception. Our website has engaged in a variety of initiatives to improve crisis services and advance suicide prevention for all, including innovative public messaging and best practices in mental health.

Our Vision

Our website is to help prevent suicide and give them a long life so that they can enjoy the precious life that God gave us and for them to appreciate it and not just waste it and to promote the rights and best interests of patients and those actually or potentially making use of psychiatric services for mental illness, including substance use disorders.


Our Mission

To prevent suicide by raising public awareness and providing education, as well as to decrease stigma and provide resources to those who have been affected by suicide.
